# Code of Conduct

Waldi is built on quiet respect.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone. We do not care about your age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual orientation. We care about how you treat people.

## Our Standards

Behavior that belongs here:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
- Respecting differing opinions and viewpoints.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Accepting responsibility when you make a mistake, apologizing, and learning from it.
- Focusing on what serves the community, not just your ego.

Behavior that will get you removed:
- Sexualized language, imagery, or advances.
- Trolling, insulting comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that fails a basic test of professional decency.

## Enforcement

The maintainer clarifies and enforces these standards. If you encounter behavior that is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful, email **me@aminzamani.com**. 

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. If you cross the line, you will be removed.

## Scope

This applies everywhere: issues, pull requests, discussions, and anywhere you officially represent the project.
